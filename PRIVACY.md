# Privacy Policy


## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy outlines the data practices for the **Bayzat Report Generator** Chrome extension (the "Extension"). Our primary goal is to provide a tool for generating PDF reports from the Bayzat platform while maintaining absolute user privacy.

## 2. No Data Collection
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.** * **Personal Information:** The Extension does not collect names, email addresses, or contact details.
* **Sensitive Data:** While the Extension reads data from your Bayzat dashboard to generate a PDF, this data is processed **locally** in your browser's memory. It is never saved, archived, or sent to any external server.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use cookies, web beacons, or analytics (such as Google Analytics) to track your behavior or usage patterns.


## 3. Required Permissions
To function, the Extension requests the following permissions from the Chrome Browser:
* **`tabs`**: Used only to access the content of the specific Bayzat page you are viewing to update the dom and add the print button.
* **`webNavigation`**: Used only to access the content of the specific Bayzat page you are viewing to update the dom and add the print button.

## 4. Third-Party Platforms
This Extension is designed to work on the Bayzat platform. 
We are not affiliated with Bayzat, and this policy does not cover Bayzat's own data practices. 
Please refer to Bayzat's Privacy Policy regarding the information you store on their platform.

## 5. Compliance with Chrome Web Store Policies
This Extension complies with the **Google Single Purpose** and **Limited Use** policies:
* We do not sell your data to third parties.
* We do not use or transfer your data for purposes unrelated to the Extension's core functionality (Generating PDFs Reports).
* We do not use your data to determine creditworthiness or for lending purposes.
